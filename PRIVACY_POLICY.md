# Privacy Policy for Flexecution

**Last Updated: January 22, 2026**

## Overview

Flexecution ("the App") is a workout tracking and planning application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection and Storage

**We do not collect any personal data.**

All information you enter into Flexecution is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

- Workout logs and exercise data
- Personal records and achievements
- Body measurements and wellness metrics
- Workout templates and scheduled workouts
- Daily notes and progress tracking

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No analytics or tracking services are used
- No advertising networks are integrated

## No Account Required

Flexecution works entirely offline. You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in to use the App.

## Data Export and Import

You have full control over your data:

- **Export**: You can export your workout data as a JSON file at any time through the Settings menu
- **Import**: You can import previously exported data
- **Delete**: You can clear all data from the App through Settings

## In-App Purchases and Subscriptions                                                            
                                                                                                   
Flexecution offers optional premium subscriptions. All payment processing is handled             
entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to             
your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials.

## Third-Party Services

Flexecution does not integrate with any third-party services. The App does not connect to:

- Cloud storage services
- Social media platforms
- Analytics providers
- Advertising networks
- Health data platforms

## Data Security

Your data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device. The security of your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

- Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
- iOS/iPadOS data protection
- App sandboxing

## Children's Privacy

Flexecution does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no mechanisms for collecting personal information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
